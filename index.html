About the artist

Melissa Slebrich

Melissa Slebrich is an emerging fine art film photographer located in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Her work beautifully contrasts the elements of nature with urban life, mirroring the tension she feels in her own experiences.

Melissa is attracted to film because of its timelessness and its ability to surprise while capturing the world as she sees it. She is particularly captivated by rich colors and striking contrasts, often portraying scenes that evoke both beauty and a sense of solitude.

This collection marks the beginning of her artistic journey, spanning from 2023, when her father gifted her his trusty Canon AE-1, to the present day. All photographs were shot on 35mm or 120mm film, and developed & scanned in Pittsburgh.
